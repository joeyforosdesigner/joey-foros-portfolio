Featured Design Projects

A curated selection of visual identities, brand concepts, and creative works that showcase my design expertise.

PROJECT 1: ZONA RECORDS

  • Industry: Rap & RnB Media
  • Objective: Build a bold and streetwear inspired visual identity that stands out from the usual trends while staying easily recognizable.
  • Creation: Complete visual identity from the logo to the graphic system.

The creative direction started with an exploration of streetwear visual codes bold typography, sharp contrasts, and minimal compositions inspired by underground culture. I wanted the identity to speak to a young, expressive audience while keeping a professional and recognizable aesthetic.

The lime green color was chosen for its high energy and visibility, balancing perfectly with black to bring a modern yet raw visual feel.

Design Details:

1- The lime green accent was used to represent vitality and creativity.
2- The black base reinforces authority and contrast.
3- The logo spacing was slightly tightened to give it a more urban feel.
4- The shapes and rhythm of the typography subtly evoke sound waves, connecting back to music.

A bold and authentic identity that visually resonates with the rap and RnB community, giving Zona Records a signature streetwear personality.

PROJECT 2: VYNE

  • Industry: Streetwear & Sports Fashion
  • Objective: Build a powerful brand image that reflects strength and high perceived value within the streetwear culture.
  • Creation: Logo design and a promotional visual series to enhance brand identity and awareness.

For Vyne, I wanted to merge the spirit of streetwear with athletic confidence. The process began with a study of strong, geometric shapes often found in sports branding. I leaned towards darker tones to convey power, ambition, and exclusivity an identity that feels premium yet approachable.

Design Details:


1- The logo was built with sharp angles to express precision and discipline.
2- The dark palette was enriched with subtle gradients to add texture and depth.
3- Promotional visuals used shadows and focused light to suggest power and momentum.
4- Repetition and symmetry in layout reinforced the idea of control and structure.



An impactful, performance-driven identity that translates the brand’s energy into every piece of visual communication.

PROJECT 3: SOLARE

  • Industry: Coffee & Relaxation Space
  • Objective: Attract coffee lovers who appreciate calm atmospheres and artistic visuals, by creating a warm and inviting brand identity.
  • Creation: Logo design and full visual identity, including packaging concepts.

Solare’s creation process revolved around warmth, comfort, and the sensory experience of good coffee. I explored color combinations that evoke calmness and optimism blue for serenity and yellow for sunlight. The logo design took inspiration from minimalist illustrations, with soft lines to create a feeling of friendliness.

Design Details:


1- Blue and yellow were balanced in proportions to avoid visual fatigue and create harmony.
2- Rounded typography conveys approachability and softness.
3- Small illustrations elements were subtly added to reinforce the brand theme.
4- The packaging was designed to feel collectible visually pleasing.

A charming and peaceful identity that captures the essence of relaxation and creativity perfectly aligned with Solare’s atmosphere.
